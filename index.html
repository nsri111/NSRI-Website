About NSRI

Noble Shivapuri Research Institute (NSRI) is a center of innovation and knowledge dedicated to advancing research and collaboration for societal impact. With a diverse team and state-of-the-art facilities, NSRI aims to address pressing challenges through evidence-based solutions and transformative research. The institute provides consultancy services to support development priorities grounded in evidence, ensuring effective planning, implementation, and evaluation. Operating amidst a dynamic development landscape, NSRI promotes good governance, poverty reduction, and social stability through strong partnerships and dedicated service. Registered in Kathmandu, Nepal, NSRI offers expertise across sectors including health, education, environment, and social sciences, all aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals. Our multidisciplinary team collaborates closely with government bodies, NGOs, and international agencies to promote best practices and sustainable development.

Our Objectives

  • Provide special technical tests focused on soil quality, purity, and strength to support infrastructure building activities with government approval.
  • Conduct research activities in social sciences including law, sociology, economics, population, environment, conflict and peace, gender studies, public health, agriculture, water and sanitation.
  • Offer consultation services in business and market sectors to foster evidence-based development.
  • Design and produce multimedia materials such as videos, documentaries, cartoons, and IEC/BCC materials to support behavior change communication.
  • Collaborate with international donors like UNDP, ILO, and UN for consultation and data verification activities.
  • Engage in economic, statistical, and computer-based data analysis to support development planning and evaluation.
  • Implement non-profit and social activities including charity, advocacy, and legal advisory services.
  • Promote formal and informal education and support establishment of educational institutions with government approval.
  • Conduct public health research, training, workshops, conferences, and advocacy for policy development.
  • Provide consultancy in physical and biological sciences with expert collaboration.
  • Deliver livelihood training and skill development programs approved by government sectors to empower communities.
