Who We Are

Noble Shivapuri Research Institute (NSRI) is a dynamic center of knowledge and innovation, driving cutting-edge research and transformative solutions to address today’s most pressing challenges. With a team of visionary experts, advanced methodologies, and state-of-the-art facilities, NSRI fosters evidence-based, multidisciplinary research that informs policy, strengthens development programs, and delivers lasting impact on society.

NSRI provides independent consultancy services in health, education, empowerment, public health engineering, market research, and development. We believe that development priorities must be evidence-based, requiring impartial planning, evaluation, and guidance to ensure effective program implementation and measurable outcomes.

Operating in Nepal’s evolving development landscape—marked by decentralized governance, private-sector growth, and regional disparities—NSRI positions itself as an effective partner in promoting good governance, poverty reduction, social harmony, and political stability. Established on 5 February 2023 and registered in Kathmandu (Company Registration No: 306540/2079/80, PAN & VAT No: 610441307), NSRI works across 16 thematic areas aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These include:

  • Health, education, and environmental research
  • Public health, social science, and market studies
  • Data verification, economics, and statistics
  • Legal advisory, policy, and governance support
  • Infrastructure quality testing and approval
  • Training, skill development, and livelihood promotion
  • Communication, advocacy, and non-profit engagement

NSRI collaborates with international donor agencies, government institutions at all levels, and community-based organizations to promote evidence-driven practices. Its platform brings together public health professionals, sociologists, scientists, policymakers, and development practitioners, combining expertise and commitment to advance knowledge and deliver meaningful social change.

Vision

To shape effective policies through evidence-based planning and implementation of result-oriented development interventions, fostered by collaboration with communities, development partners, and government institutions.

Mission

To advance evidence-based decision-making in health, education, environment, nutrition, and child and family welfare, with a focus on marginalized communities worldwide.

Scope

NSRI operates nationwide, engaging in diverse sectors including health, environment, child development, education, nutrition, social sciences, and other development priorities aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The institute’s scope further extends to raising awareness on child protection and development through social media, videography, photography, and documentary production, with a focus on reaching both national and global Nepali communities. Additionally, NSRI is committed to promoting cultural awareness, fostering global citizenship, and advancing evidence-based practices that support early learning, social and emotional development, and holistic child well-being.

Leadership

Rita Adhikari

Ms. Adhikari founded the organization in 2022 in Nepal, and it is officially registered with the Office of the Company Registrar, Kathmandu. She is a seasoned professional in communication and social mobilization, having worked with several prominent organizations including UNICEF, Nepal Fertility Care Center, WaterAid, Lifeline Nepal, FHI, Abt Associates, Blue Diamond Society, the Country Coordinating Mechanism, and ADRA Nepal. Currently, she serves as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Noble Shivapuri Research Institute Pvt. Ltd. Ms. Adhikari holds a Master’s degree from the University of Tokyo and a Bachelor’s degree from Purbanchal University, Nepal. Her expertise spans mixed-method research, big data research, Social and Behavior Change Communication, Human-Centered Design, and community mobilization. With her leadership and technical skills, she brings innovation and strategic vision to the institute’s research and development initiatives.

Kanchan Thapa

Mr. Thapa serves as the Research Uptake Manager at the Noble Shivapuri Research Institute. He holds a Master of Public Health in Epidemiology from Bangladesh and an MPhil in Population Studies from Tribhuvan University, Nepal. Over the course of his career, he has contributed to renowned organizations such as UNICEF, the Ministry of Health and Population, Lifeline Nepal, the Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST), Hope International College–Purbanchal University, the Social Development and Promotion Center (SDPC), and Tokyo Global Gateway. With more than 13 years of professional expertise, Mr. Thapa has established himself as a versatile public health professional specializing in research, Social and Behavior Change Communication, and capacity building through training of trainers.
